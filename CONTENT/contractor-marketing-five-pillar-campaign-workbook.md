# Contractor Marketing Five-Pillar Campaign Workbook

This workbook expands the five supporting content pillars for a contractor/remodeler marketing firm:

1. Better Leads, Not More Leads
2. The Referral Bridge
3. The Google Gap
4. Trust Before the Estimate
5. The AI-Ready Contractor

Use these pillars alongside the signature angle, **Projects as Content Goldmines**. The signature angle makes the firm differentiated; these supporting pillars give the firm enough campaign depth to speak to different contractor pain points, buyer stages, and service lines.

## How to use this workbook

- **Lead magnets** are designed for opt-ins, free audits, cold outreach offers, paid social CTAs, and email list building.
- **Ad angles** can be used for Facebook/Instagram, Google Search, retargeting, YouTube Shorts, cold email subject lines, or landing page sections.
- **30-day content calendars** are written for the firm’s organic social presence. They can be used on Instagram, Threads, LinkedIn, short-form video, email, or carousel posts.
- **30-day AEO website calendars** are written for the firm’s own website, with page/blog/FAQ ideas meant to capture contractor searches and answer AI-search style queries.
- **Audit and proposal snippets** can be copied into Loom audits, PDF audits, website reviews, proposals, sales pages, and consultation follow-up emails.

## Pillar: Better Leads, Not More Leads

### Positioning

Contractors usually say they want more leads, but the deeper pain is wasting time with bad-fit inquiries: tire kickers, price shoppers, ghosters, people who are not ready, people with unrealistic budgets, and shared leads sent to multiple contractors. This pillar positions the firm around lead quality, not just lead volume.

Core line: **The goal is not more calls. The goal is better calls from homeowners who understand your work, trust your proof, and are closer to hiring before you ever give an estimate.**
